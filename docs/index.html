Snow Day is the game created by everyone in the Multimedia Game Design course (MMEDIA 3L03, taught by David Ogborn) at McMaster University (Winter 2017). The player falls asleep under a wonderful and characteristic tree, only to awake in an icey and snowy labyrinth built out of the arts area of McMaster's campus. Created with Blender and Unity.
